Checkout the TIE (Today I Explored): posts of the topics in Computer Science and/or Mathematics that I focused on each day.

Our Events

Rendezvous

In these sessions we invite researchers and host a lively dialogue between students and the speakers. The conversation often begins around a central scientific theme in an informal and comfortable setting. We encourage students to ask questions, which bring out more elaborate perspectives from the discussion.

Interaction

Here we invite researchers and have free-flowing conversations with them on a variety of topics. For example, we try to understand the problems certain sections of our society are facing and how we can try to solve them. We also talk about their impressions and experiences throughout their journey in academia.

Counselling sessions

In these sessions we invite some of our professors and students to talk about the various opportunities presented by the institute, and how the students can best utilize them. In other such sessions with our alumni we also discuss the life ahead of our Integrated MSc program.

Open Mic

Open Mics provide students with an opportunity to explore their scientific interests and share them with the community. The session begins with 5-6 students briefly introducing a topic of their interest. This is followed by a discussion session, where the speakers elaborate on their topic with smaller groups of students in greater depth.
